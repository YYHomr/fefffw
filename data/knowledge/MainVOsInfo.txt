how to color text:

1: using 'currentcolor'
2: using a color that can be easy to see in both dark and light modes
-such as 
1. Blues

#1E90FF – Dodger Blue (bright, very readable)

#007BFF – Bootstrap Blue (works on light & dark)

#3399FF – Medium Blue (friendly for text/buttons)

2. Greens

#28A745 – Bright Green (good for success/status)

#00C851 – Emerald Green (stands out on both backgrounds)

#32CD32 – Lime Green (bright and clear)

3. Oranges

#FF8800 – Vibrant Orange (works on dark & light)

#FFA500 – Classic Orange (slightly softer)

#FFB347 – Light Orange (less harsh)

4. Reds

#FF4136 – Strong Red (alerts & errors)

#FF6347 – Tomato Red (slightly softer)

#FF5555 – Bright Red (high contrast)

5. Purples

#9B59B6 – Medium Purple (good for highlights)

#8E44AD – Deep Purple (standout on light backgrounds)

#B39DDB – Light Purple (clear on dark mode)

6. Neutrals (for text/buttons)

#222222 – Dark Gray (works better than black on light mode)

#EEEEEE – Light Gray (soft on dark mode)

#666666 – Medium Gray (readable anywhere)
